Raigad Fort, the 17th-century capital of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj located in Maharashtra, offers services like an aerial ropeway, guided tours, and basic accommodation. Key services include the Raigad Ropeway (4-minute transit), guided walking tours of ruins (Raj Bhavan, Durbar Hall), dining options, and lodging, available generally from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Raigad Fort Visitor Services & Information

Service Category Details & Features
Transportation Raigad Ropeway: 750m aerial tramway (4 min) to the top.
Access/Entry ~1,737 steps (trekking) or ropeway; Main Entrance: Maha Darwaja.
Guided Tours Available at the site to explore historical ruins.
Accommodation Limited lodging/hostel facilities available on top.
Dining Local vendors and restaurants offering basic meals.
Key Attractions King's Durbar, Hirakani Buruj, Jagadishwar Temple, Queen's Palace.
Operating Hours Fort: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM; Ropeway: 8:00 AM - 5:30 PM.
Entry Fee Approx. ₹10 for entry (additional costs for ropeway).
Best Time to Visit October to March (cooler weather).
